## Add Diver Well Training to Your Home Screen

Diver Well Training is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works perfectly on mobile devices. You can add it to your home screen for quick access, just like a native app!

## üçé iOS (iPhone & iPad) - Safari Browser

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone or iPad
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** You must use Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)

2. **Navigate to** your Diver Well Training website
   - Enter the URL in Safari's address bar

3. **Tap the Share Button**
   - Look for the **Share icon** (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari
   - It's located in the center-bottom of your screen

4. **Scroll Down and Tap "Add to Home Screen"**
   - Scroll through the share menu options
   - Look for the icon with a **plus sign** and text "Add to Home Screen"
   - Tap it

5. **Customize the Name** (Optional)
   - You can edit the app name if desired
   - Default name: "Diver Well"

6. **Tap "Add"**
   - The app icon will appear on your home screen

7. **Open from Home Screen**
   - Tap the icon to launch the app
   - It will open in full-screen mode, just like a native app!

## ü§ñ Android - Chrome Browser

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android device
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** Use Chrome browser (not Samsung Internet or Firefox)

2. **Navigate to** your Diver Well Training website
   - Enter the URL in Chrome's address bar

3. **Look for the Install Prompt**
   - Chrome may automatically show a banner saying "Add Diver Well to Home screen"
   - If you see this, tap **"Install"** or **"Add"**

4. **Manual Installation** (if no prompt appears):
   - Tap the **three-dot menu** (‚ãÆ) in the top-right corner
   - Select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
   - Some devices show **"Add to Home screen"** in the menu

5. **Confirm Installation**
   - Review the app name and icon
   - Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"**

6. **Open from Home Screen**
   - Find the app icon on your home screen or app drawer
   - Tap to launch - it opens in full-screen mode!

## üîß Troubleshooting

### iOS - "Add to Home Screen" Not Showing?

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome or Firefox)
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear Safari cache: Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Clear History and Website Data
- Make sure you're on the main page (not a sub-page)

### Android - Install Prompt Not Appearing?

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're using **Chrome** browser
- Check if the site is fully loaded
- Try clearing Chrome cache: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
- Manually add via menu: Three dots ‚Üí Add to Home screen

### App Not Working After Installation?

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you have an active internet connection (for first load)
- Try uninstalling and reinstalling
- Clear browser cache
- Check if your browser is up to date
