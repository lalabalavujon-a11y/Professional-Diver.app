## Upgrade and Downgrade Policies

### Upgrading Tiers

**Process:**
1. User selects new tier in account settings
2. Immediate access to new tier features on both platforms
3. Prorated charge for remainder of current billing period
4. Next billing cycle charges new tier price

**Example:**
- Current: Diver ($19.99/month), Day 15 of 30-day cycle
- Upgrade to: Company ($49.99/month)
- Charge: Prorated $15.00 for remaining 15 days
- Next bill: $49.99/month

### Downgrading Tiers

**Process:**
1. User selects lower tier in account settings
2. Access to higher-tier features removed immediately
3. Credit applied to account for unused portion
4. Next billing cycle charges new tier price

**Example:**
- Current: Company ($49.99/month), Day 15 of 30-day cycle
- Downgrade to: Diver ($19.99/month)
- Credit: $15.00 applied to account
- Next bill: $19.99/month

**Restrictions:**
- Downgrades take effect at end of current billing period
- Data from higher tier features may be archived
- Cannot downgrade if using features exclusive to current tier

## Cancellation and Refunds

### Cancellation Policy

- **Monthly subscriptions:** Cancel anytime, access continues until end of billing period
- **Annual subscriptions:** Cancel anytime, prorated refund available
- **Trial period:** Cancel anytime, no charge
- Cancellation effective at end of current billing period

### Refund Policy

- **Monthly subscriptions:** No refunds for partial months
- **Annual subscriptions:** Prorated refunds available within 30 days of purchase
- **Trial period:** No refunds (free trial)
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

### Data Retention

- User data retained for 90 days after cancellation
- Can reactivate account within 90 days to restore data
- After 90 days, data may be permanently deleted

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need separate subscriptions for Training and Network?
**A:** No. One subscription gives you access to both platforms at the same tier level.

### Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my tier?
**A:** Yes, you can change your tier anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

### Q: What happens if I cancel?
**A:** Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. You can reactivate within 90 days to restore your data.

### Q: Are there any hidden fees?
**A:** No. The price you see is the price you pay. No setup fees, no cancellation fees.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A:** Annual subscriptions are eligible for prorated refunds within 30 days. Monthly subscriptions are not refundable.

### Q: Do you offer student discounts?
**A:** Currently, we do not offer student discounts. However, the Diver tier at $19.99/month is our most affordable option.

### Q: Can I pay annually but switch to monthly later?
**A:** Yes, you can change your billing cycle anytime in your account settings.
